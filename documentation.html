GroupSaver AI Documentation

Overview

GroupSaver AI is a Gmail productivity tool that lets you save commonly emailed groups, automate follow-ups, and leverage smart AI features to streamline communication. Whether you're managing clients, team members, or event invites, GroupSaver keeps you efficient inside Gmail.

Getting Started

  1. Install the GroupSaver Gmail Add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace (coming soon).
  2. Compose an email with 2 or more recipients.
  3. Click the “Save Group” button that appears and name the group.
  4. That group will now be available in your Google Contacts for reuse.
  5. Upgrade to a paid plan to unlock more groups, AI tools, and templates.

Features

  • Save Groups — Quickly name and reuse groups of email recipients.
  • AI Suggestions — Get recommended groups based on your past emails.
  • Auto Follow-Ups — Schedule follow-up messages to specific groups.
  • Email Templates — Save and insert reusable message templates.
  • Analytics — See open rate data and usage by group (Pro/Team plans).

Plan Limitations

  • Free: 1 group, up to 3 members
  • Pro: Unlimited groups, up to 5 members per group
  • Team: 10 groups with unlimited members, then unlimited groups up to 5 members each
  • Enterprise: Unlimited everything + extra integrations

Integrations

We currently integrate with Gmail and Google Contacts. Future integrations will include:

  • Google Sheets (logging + analytics)
  • Slack (notify teams when messages go out)
  • Zapier (connect to thousands of other apps)

FAQs

  • How do I cancel? You can cancel anytime via your account dashboard.
  • Can I switch plans? Yes, upgrades/downgrades apply on your next billing cycle.
  • What’s included in the free trial? All Team features for 14 days — no card required.
  • Is my data secure? Yes, all connections are encrypted and run on Google infrastructure.
