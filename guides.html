How to Install and Use GroupSaver AI

  1. Visit our homepage and choose your plan (Pro, Team, or Enterprise).
  2. Click “Select Plan” and complete checkout using Stripe.
  3. Check your email for a confirmation and activation instructions.
  4. Follow the link to log in and start creating your groups.
  5. Use the dashboard to manage groups and access AI tools.
  6. For help, visit our Support page.
