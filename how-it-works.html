How It Works

Save Time Grouping Emails

GroupSaver AI integrates directly into your Gmail compose window. When you add multiple recipients to an email, you’ll get the option to save them as a named group. These groups are stored in your Google Contacts and can be reused anytime by typing the group name.

AI-Powered Suggestions

Our AI learns your emailing habits and will start suggesting groupings automatically. This makes it easy to build and manage your most-used teams, customers, or collaborators without repeating work.

Auto-Follow-Up

Send an email to a group and forget it. GroupSaver AI can automatically schedule follow-up emails if there’s no reply after a set time. Perfect for busy teams and sales professionals.

Simple Plans That Scale

  • Pro: Unlimited groups, up to 5 members each.
  • Team: Up to 10 groups with unlimited members, then unlimited groups with 5-member max.
  • Enterprise: Unlimited everything, plus advanced API access.
